Yu Bai

 

About me: I am a fourth-year PhD student in Statistics at Stanford University (specializing in statistical learning theories and non-convex optimization), where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. John Duchi. My research interest lies broadly in the span of machine learning, optimization, and statistics. I am particularly interested in deep generative models, non-convex optimization, and principled approaches for deep learning. Here is my CV.

I am delighted to complement my research with industrial experiences. I spent a wonderful summer at Google Research in 2016 working with Li Zhang. Currently (summer 2018) I am working as a research intern at Amazon AI in Palo Alto.

Prior to Stanford, I was an undergrad in mathematics at Peking University.
