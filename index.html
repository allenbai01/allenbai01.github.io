Yu Bai

 

About me: I am a Research Scientist at Salesforce Research. In September 2019, I completed my PhD in Statistics at Stanford University (specializing in machine learning), where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. John Duchi. I was a member of the Statistical Machine Learning Group. During my PhD, I also spent two summers in research labs in the industry: at Google Research in 2016, and at Amazon AI Palo Alto in 2018. Here is my (irregularly updated) CV.

My research interest lies broadly in machine learning. My recent focus is on the theoretical foundations and principled algorithm designs for deep learning and reinforcement learning.

Prior to Stanford, I was an undergrad in mathematics at Peking University.
